AI & Analytics

I’m a Business Data Analyst passionate about transforming complex datasets into meaningful business insights. With experience managing teams and projects at the University of Connecticut and Tata Consultancy Services, I’ve honed my ability to connect data, strategy, and execution.

At UConn, I led analytical initiatives that improved workforce efficiency and budget utilization, while collaborating with cross-functional teams of over 20 professionals to align analytics with operational goals. At TCS, I supported large-scale financial projects for the State Bank of India, automating data pipelines and developing dashboards that streamlined reporting for leadership across portfolios worth over $10M.

Beyond traditional analytics, I’ve built an AI-powered resume system using generative AI, designed to intelligently match candidate profiles to job opportunities. My portfolio also includes predictive modeling of overdose deaths (InsightRx) and global happiness analysis, reflecting a deep interest in combining analytics, social impact, and innovation. Driven by curiosity and precision, I focus on turning raw data into clear, actionable insights that help organizations make smarter, faster decisions.
